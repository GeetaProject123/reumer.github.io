Academic Project In B.tech

Iot Based Anti-Smuggling System For Trees

A technological solution designed to combat illegal logging and tree smuggling. By deploying sensors and IOT devices across forested areas , this system monitors tree movement ,environmental conditions, and unauthorized activities in real- time. It employs algorithms to detect suspicious patterns ,triggering immediate alerts to authorities upon detecting potential illegal activities.With geo-fencing capabilities and robust communication infrastructure.
